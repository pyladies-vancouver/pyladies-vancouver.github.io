## Speak

We're always looking for speakers at our meetup! No experience necessary. Whether you're
speaking for the first time, or a seasoned presenter, we welcome you in our community.

See the [Speak at our meetup](/speak/) guide for talk formats, how
to prepare, and what to expect.

{{< button url="https://sessionize.com/pyladies-vancouver-meetup/" label="Submit your talk idea" icon="fa-solid fa-microphone-lines" >}}

## Host

If you have an event space in Vancouver, and can host our next meetup, we'd love to hear
from you! A space for 20-30 people with a projector would be great. We're also looking for
a space that is accessible by public transit.

See [Host a venue](/host/) for what hosting involves.

{{< button url="mailto:vancouver@pyladies.com?subject=Hosting%20a%20PyLadies%20Vancouver%20meetup" label="Offer a space" variant="outline" icon="fa-solid fa-location-dot" >}}

## Donate & sponsor

PyLadies Vancouver is run entirely by volunteers, and donations help us keep our meetups
free and welcoming. You can also sponsor a specific event, most commonly by covering food
(like pizza) for an evening. See [Donate and sponsor](/donate/) for
the details.

{{< button url="https://psfmember.org/civicrm/contribute/transact/?reset=1&id=60" label="Donate to PyLadies Vancouver" variant="secondary" icon="fa-solid fa-heart" >}}

## Help organize

Organizing our events is a team effort, and there's room for lots of hands: coordinating
with venues and sponsors, supporting speakers, MCing, writing blog recaps, or designing
posters. Most of these don't need any special access or accounts. See
[Help organize](/organize/) for the full list.

Just tell us in the **#city-vancouver** channel on the PyLadies Slack what you'd like to
help with, and we'll get you started. No experience required.
