PyLadies Vancouver is proud to collaborate along with this community initiative!

PyLadies Vancouver reside and share knowledge on the unceded territories of the xʷməθkwəy̓əm, Skwxwú7mesh, Stó:lō and Səl̓ílwətaʔ/Selilwitulh Nations. We encourage participation from members from Indigenous communities across Canada.

Are you looking for professional development and portfolio-building opportunities in the data science sector? Join data science enthusiasts for the Vancouver DataJam: a virtual event with a focus on supporting members of gender and racial minorities get their careers in data science kickstarted.

Learn about our event: https://www.vancouverdatajam.ca/

The event will be hosted 100% virtually from September 18-25 2021. The event starts with workshops in R, Python, GitHub, and more. Participants will then have the opportunity to gain hands on experience in data science via a series of projects. Join the Vancouver DataJam either as a participant or team lead!

Learn about our workshops in Python, R and GitHub: https://www.vancouverdatajam.ca/workshops

If you want to lead
The project call for proposals is open for data-science project ideas tackling COVID19 recovery at the social, public health, economic and sustainable dimensions.
