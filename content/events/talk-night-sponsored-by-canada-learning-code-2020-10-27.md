Talks

4 Tips to Visualize Behavior With Python Matplotlib (Ha Dinh)
- Ha is currently working at Unbounce as a Senior Data Analyst, supporting the organization to understand its customers and make data-informed decisions.

Exploration of Geospatial data and how to visualize it with Kepler (Duong Vu)
- Duong is part of the Data Science team at UrbanLogiq, a Vancouver startup that leverage urban computing to help government agencies in Canada and US better understand their communities.

Comparing Snakes and Gems (Holly Becker)
I recently learned Ruby and was surprised at how different it was from Python, considering they're very similar on the surface. Let's dig into some of these details!
- Holly is a Pythonista recently transplanted into Ruby. She has more hobbies than she has time for, including running Linux, board games, birdwatching, and reading fantasy.
