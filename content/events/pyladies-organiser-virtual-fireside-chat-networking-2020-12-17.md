Say a cosy goodbye to 2020, and hello to 2021 with PyLadies Vancouver past and present, Seattle, Brazil and our venue sponsor Cisco!

We're recruiting more volunteer organizers! Join our panel of PyLadies Organizers past and present from around the world to discuss what it's like to organize Pyladies meetups, to find yourself in our stories, and ask 2020’s burning question: how has the move to virtual worked? Got questions you'd like to ask? Submit them here! https://app.sli.do/event/zsgjrlzu

We'll be launching both our recruitment drive AND A COMPETITION for you, the PyLadies Vancouver community to have a say in what we look like going forward! And maybe win some prizes while you're at it. We're still working on that ;)

We haven't even shared THE BEST PART! We'll be wrapping up the evening with a new format of networking event, using proximity chat ( gather.town ) ! We'll have a virtual (and very easy to join/set up) space where you can enter and join conversations based on where you are. Just like we used to do in person! Thank you to our sponsor Cisco for helping this happen!
