PyCascades is a regional Python conference in the Pacific Northwest, celebrating the west coast Python developer and user community. Our organizing team includes members of the Vancouver, Seattle, and Portland Python user groups. Started in 2018, PyCascades features two days of single track Python conference.
In 2024 PyCascades will return to Seattle and PyLadies Vancouver community are encouraged to participate.

PyCascades team will be joining us to tell us about their conference and their Call for Proposals.
