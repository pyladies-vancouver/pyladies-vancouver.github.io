Schedule:
6:00 Doors open, networking/socializing
6:30 Vote on and watch talk 1
7:00 Discuss talk 1
7:15 Vote on and watch talk 2
7:45 Discuss talk 1
8:00 Vote on and watch talk 3
8:30 Discuss talk 3
9:00 Wrap up
