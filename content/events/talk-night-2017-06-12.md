A night of talks from our community! We have a couple talks lined up, but are looking for more speakers. If you're interested in giving a talk on anything interesting to a group of Pythonistas, please contact us or leave a comment. We love first-time speakers!

- If I can become a speaker, so can you - Seb Vetter

- Can I use that code? On software licenses - Holly Becker

- Python & AWS - Julie Sturgeon

- Python & Django at Copperleaf - Serena & Anna

Dinner will be provided, sponsored by Copperleaf.

Please take a moment to read our Code of Conduct before you attend the event:
https://www.meetup.com/PyLadies-Vancouver/about/

Thank you to Copperleaf for sponsoring the space and food!

If you're driving, the entrance is off Hebb Ave. Turn left (the gate should block going straight) and park anywhere on P3 that isn't reserved. Thomas from Copperleaf will be standing at the door, and can take you around the corner and up the elevator into the office. You'll have to pay for parking.
