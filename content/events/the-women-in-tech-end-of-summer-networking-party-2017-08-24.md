Want to meet some other amazing women in tech? Several meetups are teaming up for an end of summer networking party.

Tickets are $5 (to pay for snacks/drinks), and you have to reserve them via the Eventbrite page: https://www.eventbrite.ca/e/the-women-in-tech-end-of-summer-networking-party-tickets-36527659212 RSVP'ing here will not count!

The event will run from 5:30 - 7:30 pm -- feel free to show up anytime!

Organized by WIMDI, more details on their event (https://www.meetup.com/Vancouver-Women-in-Male-Dominated-Industries/events/242014176/):

"Summer time is all about fun times, so we're going to take a hiatus from our Speaker Series for the month of August and do a fun networking party instead.

There are so many amazing groups for women in tech in Vancouver, and it's about time we all got together for some fun! This event is a partnership between some of Vancouver's best Women in Tech groups in the city, including:

- Women in Male-Dominated Industries (https://www.meetup.com/Vancouver-Women-in-Male-Dominated-Industries/)
- The Women's Leadership Collective (https://www.meetup.com/Womens-Leadership-Collective/)
- Women Can Tech (https://www.meetup.com/Women-Can-Tech-YVR/)
- WWEST
- Tech Ladies

Come on out for a fun night of networking, drinks, and snacks where you can meet other women in similar fields, make professional connections, and find some new friends who get where you're coming from."
