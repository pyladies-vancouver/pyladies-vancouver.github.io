Join us for a casual non-technical outdoor picnic with fellow PyLadies in one of Vancouver's beautiful city park. Family, friends, and furfriends are welcome to join. 😊

Please bring something to share! either cooked food, store-bought food, or food for grilling.

We will provide 2 portable BBQ grills: one will be dedicated for grilling food for those with special dietary needs.

Please bring your own lawn chair, plate and utensils.

Food sign up: https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1m0h7Lo1SGLJLQiBOJ1Wk1xVkDspkaWrDW8AFvLYAlIY/edit?usp=sharing

Location:

Plan A 🌞: We meet in Trout Lake Beach, at the south end of Trout Lake in John Hendry Park at Victoria Dr and East 19th Ave, Vancouver.

Plan B ⛈: Use the sheltered area at Trout lake park, or move to the community centre.

Time: 4:30 PM - end

How to find us: Look for PyLadies Vancouver signage

Looking forward to meet you all!

We expect everyone attending our meetups to abide by our Code of Conduct (http://www.meetup.com/PyLadies-Vancouver/about/).
