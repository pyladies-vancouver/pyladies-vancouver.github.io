Big thanks to SFU Food Systems lab for being our Zoom sponsor for this workshop!

You have a vague idea what the Cloud is, you’ve heard of DevOps. Supposedly Python is great at these and you want to know more!

Join Cisco Senior Cloud Software Engineer, PyLadies co-organiser and Canada Learning Code instructor Meghan Halton for a deep-ish dive into the technologies behind the modern world wide web.

Get hands on with the internet and set up a small server on your laptop to serve your python applications. Learn how you can take this beyond the laptop and into the cloud in this 2 part workshop with PyLadies Vancouver.
