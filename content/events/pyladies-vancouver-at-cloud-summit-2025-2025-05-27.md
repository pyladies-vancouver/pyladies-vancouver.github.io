PyLadies Vancouver will have a community booth at Cloud Summit 2025, and we’d love your help getting the word out! 🎉

Stop by our booth and chat with our volunteers (Mariatta, Ada, Amanda, and Alana) to learn about PyLadies Vancouver community.

**Note**: You must buy a ticket through the Cloud Summit website to attend the event.

🗓️ Date: May 27, 2025
🕛 Time: 11:00 AM onward
📍 Location: The Orpheum Theatre, Vancouver
**🎟️ Tickets: [https://lu.ma/cloudsummit25](https://lu.ma/cloudsummit25)**

Cloud Summit is Western Canada’s leading cloud tech event, and this year’s lineup is truly next-level:

🌟 Featured Speakers:

* Matt Biilmann, CEO of Netlify
* Eric Johnson, Principal Developer Advocate at AWS

💡 Here’s what’s going down:

* 16 talks across 2 stages
* AWS-led hands-on workshops
* Interactive activities & games
* Sponsor booths (swing by ours!)
* A high-energy closing party to celebrate and network!

**New: PyLadies Vancouver Tote Bag**
Show your support to our community! With a minimum contribution of to PyLadies Vancouver, you'll receive a PyLadies Vancouver tote bag which you can pick up at our booth.
Limited quantity!
