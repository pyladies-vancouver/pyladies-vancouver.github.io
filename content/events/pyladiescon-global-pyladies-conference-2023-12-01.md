**📢 Attention Community Members! Join Us at PyLadiesCon 🐍🌟**

Get ready for something inspiring! We're thrilled to announce the upcoming PyLadies Conference (PyLadiesCon), an online and FREE event dedicated to empowering individuals, fostering learning, and promoting diversity within the Python community. 🎉

**Conference website is now live**

Check the schedule and register at [https://conference.pyladies.com](https://conference.pyladies.com)

**🗓️ Save the Date: December 1st - 3rd, 2023**

At PyLadiesCon, we're bringing the Python world to your doorstep. Mark your calendars for the first weekend of December, when we'll come together for an unforgettable two-day experience.

Here's what you can look forward to:

📚 **Insightful Talks:** Dive deep into Python's latest trends, best practices, and cutting-edge technologies with our inspiring speakers.

🎙️ **Engaging Panels:** Join thought-provoking discussions on topics ranging from Python development to career growth and beyond.

🤝 **Collaborative Networking:** Connect with fellow Python enthusiasts, expand your professional network, and forge new friendships.

Stay tuned for more updates on how you can get involved and contribute to this remarkable event. Together, we'll make PyLadiesCon an unforgettable celebration of Python excellence.

Talks will be presented live through the online platform, Hubilo.

Take advantage of this opportunity to learn, connect, and empower one another. Join us at PyLadiesCon and participate in Python's bright and diverse future. 🌈💻🚀
